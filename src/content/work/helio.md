summary: How we cut time-to-insight by 71% by killing the dropdown.
problem: Customers were running the same five queries every Monday morning and abandoning the platform when the loading state stretched past three seconds. The dashboard was technically powerful but emotionally exhausting.
approach: Shadowed 12 analyst-users for two weeks. Found the actual job was "answer this question for my standup in 90 seconds". Re-anchored the entire IA around saved questions instead of saved queries.
outcome: 71% reduction in median time-to-insight. Mon-AM session length down 40%, NPS up 28 pts. The team renamed Saved Queries to Questions; the rename stuck.
