For this project, I collaborated with two dedicated designers who handled the full UI/UX execution. My role focused on building the foundation and consistency of the product:

- **Design:** Organizing the design layout
- **Design System Setup:** Built a scalable system covering typography, color palette, spacing, grids, and reusable UI components. Each component was designed with clear states (default, hover, active, disabled, error, success) to guarantee predictable and consistent interactions.
- **Responsiveness:** Ensured smooth performance across desktop, tablet, and mobile breakpoints.
- **Iteration & Client Feedback:** Supported layout adjustments and refined UI elements in response to customer review sessions.
- **Testing & QA:** Conducted website testing to validate usability, component behavior, and responsiveness before handoff to development.
