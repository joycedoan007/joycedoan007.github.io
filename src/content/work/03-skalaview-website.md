<div style="margin: 0 0 20px;">
  <div class="overline" style="color: var(--text-accent); margin-bottom: 8px;">01 — Context</div>
  <h2 style="font: 600 22px/1.2 'Poppins', sans-serif; letter-spacing: -0.02em; color: var(--text-bright); margin: 0;">The Challenge</h2>
</div>

Skalaview is a digital marketing and e-commerce company that uses generative AI to help brands grow their online presence, automate sales processes, and engage customers more effectively. Their platform combines AI Assistants, AI Performance Marketing, and AI Insights into a single offering — serving businesses across retail, healthcare, and real estate in Vietnam and Singapore.

The challenge was translating a technically sophisticated AI platform into a website that business owners could immediately understand and trust. The risk on both sides was real: too much technical language and you lose the SME decision-maker; too much marketing language and you lose credibility with the tech-evaluating buyer.

<div style="background: var(--bg-surface); border-left: 2px solid var(--primary-500); border-radius: 0 var(--radius-lg) var(--radius-lg) 0; padding: 20px 24px; margin: 28px 0 56px;">
  <div class="overline" style="color: var(--text-accent); margin-bottom: 8px;">My Role</div>
  <p style="font: 400 15px/26px 'Poppins', sans-serif; color: var(--text-primary); margin: 0;">I was part of the in-house design team at Skalaview, working alongside marketing, development, and the founding team. My work covered the full design surface: brand identity, UI design, design system setup, and Webflow execution — including the bilingual (English and Vietnamese) site experience.</p>
</div>

<div style="margin: 0 0 20px;">
  <div class="overline" style="color: var(--text-accent); margin-bottom: 8px;">02 — Discovery</div>
  <h2 style="font: 600 22px/1.2 'Poppins', sans-serif; letter-spacing: -0.02em; color: var(--text-bright); margin: 0;">Research & Key Insights</h2>
</div>

AI product marketing operates differently from standard SaaS. Four patterns shaped the design direction.

<div style="display: grid; grid-template-columns: 1fr 1fr; gap: 12px; margin: 24px 0 56px;">
  <div style="background: var(--bg-surface); border: 1px solid var(--border-subtle); border-radius: var(--radius-xl); padding: 24px;">
    <div style="font: 600 11px/16px 'JetBrains Mono', monospace; color: var(--text-accent); margin-bottom: 10px;">01</div>
    <p style="font: 600 14px/20px 'Poppins', sans-serif; color: var(--text-bright); margin: 0 0 8px;">SMEs need outcomes, not features</p>
    <p style="font: 400 13px/22px 'Poppins', sans-serif; color: var(--text-secondary); margin: 0;">Small and medium business owners don't evaluate AI capabilities — they evaluate whether the product will help them sell more, save time, or find customers. Feature-first copy loses them immediately.</p>
  </div>
  <div style="background: var(--bg-surface); border: 1px solid var(--border-subtle); border-radius: var(--radius-xl); padding: 24px;">
    <div style="font: 600 11px/16px 'JetBrains Mono', monospace; color: var(--text-accent); margin-bottom: 10px;">02</div>
    <p style="font: 600 14px/20px 'Poppins', sans-serif; color: var(--text-bright); margin: 0 0 8px;">Trust is built through specificity</p>
    <p style="font: 400 13px/22px 'Poppins', sans-serif; color: var(--text-secondary); margin: 0;">Vague AI claims ("cutting-edge," "revolutionary") are everywhere and signal nothing. Concrete use cases — inventory automation, appointment scheduling, customer support — are what make the platform feel real and applicable.</p>
  </div>
  <div style="background: var(--bg-surface); border: 1px solid var(--border-subtle); border-radius: var(--radius-xl); padding: 24px;">
    <div style="font: 600 11px/16px 'JetBrains Mono', monospace; color: var(--text-accent); margin-bottom: 10px;">03</div>
    <p style="font: 600 14px/20px 'Poppins', sans-serif; color: var(--text-bright); margin: 0 0 8px;">Bilingual isn't just translation</p>
    <p style="font: 400 13px/22px 'Poppins', sans-serif; color: var(--text-secondary); margin: 0;">Vietnamese and English audiences have different expectations around formality, hierarchy, and trust signals. The bilingual site needed to feel native in both — not just a direct word swap between versions.</p>
  </div>
  <div style="background: var(--bg-surface); border: 1px solid var(--border-subtle); border-radius: var(--radius-xl); padding: 24px;">
    <div style="font: 600 11px/16px 'JetBrains Mono', monospace; color: var(--text-accent); margin-bottom: 10px;">04</div>
    <p style="font: 600 14px/20px 'Poppins', sans-serif; color: var(--text-bright); margin: 0 0 8px;">Partnership framing over vendor framing</p>
    <p style="font: 400 13px/22px 'Poppins', sans-serif; color: var(--text-secondary); margin: 0;">Skalaview's positioning is explicitly about partnership — "more than choosing a service provider." The visual and content system had to reinforce that tone consistently, not undercut it with transactional UI patterns.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="margin: 0 0 20px;">
  <div class="overline" style="color: var(--text-accent); margin-bottom: 8px;">03 — Design</div>
  <h2 style="font: 600 22px/1.2 'Poppins', sans-serif; letter-spacing: -0.02em; color: var(--text-bright); margin: 0;">Key Design Decisions</h2>
</div>

<div style="display: flex; flex-direction: column; gap: 10px; margin: 24px 0 56px;">
  <div style="border-left: 2px solid var(--primary-500); background: var(--bg-surface); border-radius: 0 var(--radius-xl) var(--radius-xl) 0; padding: 24px 28px;">
    <div class="overline" style="color: var(--text-accent); margin-bottom: 8px;">Decision 01</div>
    <p style="font: 600 15px/1.4 'Poppins', sans-serif; color: var(--text-bright); margin: 0 0 10px;">Lead with outcomes, not AI terminology</p>
    <p style="font: 400 14px/24px 'Poppins', sans-serif; color: var(--text-primary); margin: 0;">The hero and service sections were framed around what the business gains — improved visibility, streamlined sales, better customer engagement — rather than the underlying AI technology. "Customized marketing solutions" and "streamlining sales processes" land faster for an SME owner than any model name or technical spec.</p>
  </div>
  <div style="border-left: 2px solid var(--primary-500); background: var(--bg-surface); border-radius: 0 var(--radius-xl) var(--radius-xl) 0; padding: 24px 28px;">
    <div class="overline" style="color: var(--text-accent); margin-bottom: 8px;">Decision 02</div>
    <p style="font: 600 15px/1.4 'Poppins', sans-serif; color: var(--text-bright); margin: 0 0 10px;">Modular design system for bilingual content</p>
    <p style="font: 400 14px/24px 'Poppins', sans-serif; color: var(--text-primary); margin: 0;">A modular component system was critical for the bilingual build — Vietnamese text is typically shorter in character count but denser in meaning, while English can run longer in UI-sensitive spots. Every component was designed to absorb both language variants without breaking layout or hierarchy.</p>
  </div>
  <div style="border-left: 2px solid var(--primary-500); background: var(--bg-surface); border-radius: 0 var(--radius-xl) var(--radius-xl) 0; padding: 24px 28px;">
    <div class="overline" style="color: var(--text-accent); margin-bottom: 8px;">Decision 03</div>
    <p style="font: 600 15px/1.4 'Poppins', sans-serif; color: var(--text-bright); margin: 0 0 10px;">Industry verticals as navigation anchors</p>
    <p style="font: 400 14px/24px 'Poppins', sans-serif; color: var(--text-primary); margin: 0;">Rather than organizing by product alone, the site uses industry context (retail, healthcare, real estate) to help prospects self-identify. A healthcare business evaluating AI assistants needs to see their own use case — appointment scheduling, patient queries — not a generic feature list.</p>
  </div>
  <div style="border-left: 2px solid var(--primary-500); background: var(--bg-surface); border-radius: 0 var(--radius-xl) var(--radius-xl) 0; padding: 24px 28px;">
    <div class="overline" style="color: var(--text-accent); margin-bottom: 8px;">Decision 04</div>
    <p style="font: 600 15px/1.4 'Poppins', sans-serif; color: var(--text-bright); margin: 0 0 10px;">Clear visual hierarchy for data-driven content</p>
    <p style="font: 400 14px/24px 'Poppins', sans-serif; color: var(--text-primary); margin: 0;">Skalaview's platform generates insights and analytics — the website needed to reflect that capability through confident, structured layout. Dense information was organized through clear typographic hierarchy, section rhythm, and whitespace so data-heavy sections felt structured rather than overwhelming.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="margin: 48px 0 20px;">
  <div class="overline" style="color: var(--text-accent); margin-bottom: 8px;">06 — Outcome</div>
  <h2 style="font: 600 22px/1.2 'Poppins', sans-serif; letter-spacing: -0.02em; color: var(--text-bright); margin: 0;">Result</h2>
</div>

Skalaview launched with a bilingual website that communicates a technically sophisticated AI platform in terms that SME decision-makers can immediately evaluate and act on. The modular system supports ongoing content expansion across product pages, industry verticals, and both language variants without requiring design rework.

The partnership framing — reinforced through tone, layout, and the About and Partnerships pages — gave the brand a distinctive position in a market where AI vendors typically lead with technology rather than relationship.

<div style="background: var(--bg-surface); border: 1px solid var(--border-default); border-radius: var(--radius-xl); padding: 28px 32px; margin-top: 28px;">
  <div class="overline" style="color: var(--text-accent); margin-bottom: 12px;">Reflection</div>
  <p style="font: 400 15px/26px 'Poppins', sans-serif; color: var(--text-primary); margin: 0;">The bilingual requirement was the most technically demanding part of the project — not the translation itself, but ensuring every component held together in both languages without layout drift. Building the modular system before touching any page-level design was the right call. Without that foundation, the bilingual build would have been unmanageable. If I were doing this again, I'd involve the Vietnamese copywriter earlier in the process so language constraints informed component sizing from the start, rather than being handled as an adaptation step.</p>
</div>
